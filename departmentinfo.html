Physics Department

Department Information

The department of Physics was established in 1950. This department deals with curriculum in Engineering Physics and Applied Physics common to all branches of first year engineering and polytechnic

Besides teaching activity the staff members of this department are involved in teaching of some materials like alloy, cement, lime, alum, bleaching powder, water and aggregates. Also coordinate first year admission process & other activities of the collage.

In the last five years the faculty members of the department have contributed more than 30 research papers in international journals of repute and international conference proceedings.

RESEARCH

Besides teaching activity the staff members of the department are involved in research of nano material by various techniques for sensors.

Other areas of research include Embedded System for Gas Sensing, 3 Phase Induction Motor Control with redundancy techniques, Super capacitors, Fuel Cells, Detection of soil parameters using WSN technology, etc.
